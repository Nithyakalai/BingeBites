ABOUT US

Welcome to BingeBites Café

BingeBites is where the comfort of a café meets the magic of cinema. Designed for movie lovers, food enthusiasts, and cozy hangouts, we offer a one-of-a-kind private screening experience paired with freshly crafted food and beverages.

At BingeBites, guests can book private screening lounges to watch a curated selection of films while simultaneously enjoying full café service — without interruptions.

Whether it’s a cozy date night, a friends’ binge session, or a private celebration, our lounges are designed to offer premium visuals, immersive sound, and a relaxed café ambience.

Our café menu blends Indian flavors with modern café classics, allowing you to order snacks, beverages, desserts, and curated movie combos directly to your lounge — so the experience never pauses.

BingeBites isn’t just about watching movies. It’s about creating memories, conversations, and moments — all while sipping your favorite brew and enjoying great food.
